About

Founded in 2015 by a group of students, the Berkeley Barbell Club was created for this sole purpose: to encompass all strength training and bring together passionate UC Berkeley students in a tight-knit community.

If you're interested in powerlifting, olympic lifting, strong man or bodybuilding join the Berkeley Barbell Club!
